Problem statement:
Interactive learning is a crucial part of early development of language based skills to ensure full retention and proper communication/vocabulary. This project is a simple but fun way to learn the names of the states of India.

Scope of the project: This program has applications in education, entertainment, healthcare and communication. It is the basis of many popular online word games and gameshow challenges. It is a fun and interactive way to teach vocabulary and proper diction of a language to beginners. It's a nostalgic and easy way for stroke victims or patients suffering from brain damage to regain their communication skills.


Target users: Children, adolescents or any individual seeking to better their basic language skills or simply looking for a fun way to pass the time.
